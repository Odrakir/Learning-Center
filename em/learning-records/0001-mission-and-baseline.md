Ricardo is 0–12 months into his first EM role at Goodnotes, managing a small
(2–4) team spanning **iOS (his own background), Backend, and QA (neither of which
he has practised)**. He's motivated by long-term mastery, not a current crisis,
and cares about all four EM pillars (growing people, delivery, team health,

people whose craft he cannot evaluate by reading their code*. Favour

outcome/legibility thinking) over technical depth. He's open to all learning
styles, so lessons can mix frameworks, scenarios, primary sources, and drills.
